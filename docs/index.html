Our Story

Where Prosper Gathers

Born from Prosper's storied Cotton Gin Café heritage, The Gin is the go-to destination for families, friends, and date nights alike. We serve Texas comfort food with a modern twist — from wood-fired Neapolitan pizza to Texas Wagyu burgers — all day, every day.

Whether you're here for weekend brunch, late-night pizza, or just a cold pint and great company, our doors are open. Great food, crafted cocktails, and the kind of atmosphere that keeps you coming back.

Located in the heart of downtown Prosper, we've become the neighborhood's living room — where good times happen every single day.
